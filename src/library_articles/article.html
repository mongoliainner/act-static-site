1/ speed adjustment

Use video editing software (like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro,
or iMovie) to increase the playback speed of your footage. Typically,
you can find an option to adjust speed or durationâ€”set it to 200%
or higher for noticeable fast motion

2/ time remapping

For more control, use time remapping features available in
advanced editing software. This allows you to create variable speed
changes within the same clip, enabling sections to play faster or
slower as needed.

3/ frame rate consideration

If shooting your own footage, consider filming at a higher frame
rate (e.g., 60 fps or 120 fps) and then playing it back at a standard
frame rate (30 fps). This will give you smooth fast motion when you
speed up the footage in post-production
